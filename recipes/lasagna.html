Lasagna

Description

Worlds best lasagna recipe. With nearly 20,000 reviews, this recipe is guaranteed to leave everyone wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28oz) can crushed tomatos
  • 2 (6oz) can tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5oz) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 4 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 lb mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In Dutch oven, cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until well browned.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water.
  3. Season with sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, 1 tsp salt, pepper, and 2tbsp parsley.
  4. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally
  5. Bring a large pot of slightly salted water to a boil.
  6. Cook lasagna noodles ni boiling water for 8-10 minutes
  7. Drain noodles, rinse with cold water
  8. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining parsley, and 1/2 tsp salt.
  9. Preheat oven to 375F
  10. To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups meat sauce in bottom of 13x9 baking dish
  11. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce.
  12. Spread with 1/2 ricotta mixture
  13. Top with 1/3 of mozzarella cheese slices
  14. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
  15. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  16. Cover with foil
    17. * to prevent sticking spray foil with cooking spray
  17. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes
  18. Remove foil and bake additional 25min
  19. Cool 15 minutes before serving
