Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description

A childhood classic, the good old PB & J. One of the first things a kid learns to make for themselves...or at least, it used to be. Haha! You can change this up in a variety of different ways as to your preference.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
    • * Choose your favorite: white, wheat, rye, sourdough, etc
  • Peanut butter
    • * If allergic to peanuts, use preferred such as sun butter
  • Jelly
    • * Choose your favorite: Jelly, Jam, or Preserves
    * Choose your favorite flavor: Grape, Strawberry, Blackberry, Mixed Berry, etc.
  • Butter knife
  • Plate

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients
  2. Place 2 slices of bread on your plate next to each other
  3. Using the butter knife, spread the peanut butter/sun butter over one side of the slice of bread
  4. Clean the knife
  5. Spread jelly over one side of the 2nd slice of bread
  6. Place peanut butter slice of bread, peanut butter side down, on top of the jelly-side up sliced bread
  7. Cut sandwich into half in squares or triangles if you please
  8. Enjoy!
